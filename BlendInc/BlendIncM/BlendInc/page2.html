Terms & Conditions

-Instagram Data Download

Log in to your Instagram account. Go to your profile and click on the three horizontal lines (menu icon) in the top right corner. Select "Settings" from the menu. Scroll down and click on "Security." Under "Data and History," select "Download Data." Enter your email address and password to request a download link. Instagram will email you a link to download your data, which typically includes photos, comments, profile information, and more.

-LinkedIn Data Download

Log in to your LinkedIn account. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner and select "Settings & Privacy" from the dropdown menu. In the left sidebar, click on "Privacy" and then select "How LinkedIn uses your data." Scroll down to the "Getting a copy of your data" section and click on "Download your data." LinkedIn will guide you through the process of selecting the data categories you want to download, such as connections, messages, posts, and more. After selecting your data preferences, LinkedIn will email you a download link to retrieve your data.

--Ensure to review and comply with Instagram's and LinkedIn's terms of service and data usage policies while downloading and using data from these platforms.
